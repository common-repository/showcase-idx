== Installation ==

1. **Sign up for an account at [Showcase IDX](https://showcaseidx.com/pricing/?utm_source=WordPress.org&utm_medium=referral&utm_campaign=plugin-page).**
2. Once you have an account with Showcase IDX, we will go through a paperless MLS confirmation process to confirm your valid MLS membership.
3. After validating your MLS membership, you will receive a welcome email with instructions on how to download, install, and validate your Showcase IDX plugin.

**Installing in WordPress:**
1. The simplest way to install is to click on 'Plugins', then 'Add New', and type 'Showcase IDX' in the search field. Navigate to our page and click 'Install Now'. WordPress will handle the rest.
2. You should see a message stating that the plugin was installed successfully. Click the 'Activate Plugin' link.
3. Navigate to 'Showcase IDX' which can be found near the bottom of the left navigation in your WordPress dashboard.
4. Click the button to activate your site.
5. You can now set up your default search page and start using our shortcodes to add hotsheets and other widgets to your site.
6. Sign up for an account at [Showcase IDX](https://showcaseidx.com/free-trial/idx-free-trial/?utm_source=WordPress.org&utm_medium=referral&utm_campaign=plugin-page). Once you have an account, we'll again go through a paperless MLS confirmation process.
7. After we've validated your MLS membership, we'll send you a welcome email with instructions to activate your Showcase IDX.
